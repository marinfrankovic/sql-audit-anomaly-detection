Seeing — and understanding — who touches your data

AI-Augmented SQL Audit & Behaviour Analytics · What we showed you today
“Can we see who accessed which database records, when and from where — and know whether the behaviour was normal or abnormal, even when the person has legitimate elevated access?” — Yes.

Every access to your banking databases (both cloud and server-based) is recorded in one central place. On top of that log we run three layers of detection that work together — and an AI that explains what it found in plain language. It all runs inside your Azure tenant, on synthetic data, ready to show on day one.

Three layers of detection — because rules alone aren’t enough

LAYER 1 · RULES

Known-bad patterns

Fixed, explainable rules: a break-glass account being used, a DELETE on a money-transfer table, changes to permissions, sensitive data read after hours.

Reliable and auditable. Catches what we already know is dangerous.

LAYER 2 · MACHINE LEARNING

Never-seen-before behaviour

The system learns each user’s normal pattern over 90 days and flags statistical outliers — like a sudden burst of activity — without any rule being written.

Even a trusted DBA is measured against their own normal.

LAYER 3 · AI

Explains the “why”

AI reads the evidence and explains, in plain language, why something is suspicious and what to check first — grounded strictly in the audit records, never invented.

Turns raw signals into an analyst-ready story in seconds.

What you saw

  • A single dashboard with 90 days of history, already populated.
  • Executive KPIs: total access events, behaviour anomalies, high-risk anomalies, break-glass use.
  • All three detection layers side by side, each clearly labelled.
  • An Investigation view that ranks activity by risk score, with full evidence (user, time, object, statement, source IP).
  • Email alerts for high-risk events, visible in Azure Monitor → Alerts.

Why it matters

Insider & privileged-user risk: spot misuse even by people who are allowed to access the data.
Faster investigations: AI explanations cut triage from hours to minutes.
Compliance evidence: a complete, queryable record of access to sensitive financial data.

How it works (in one line)

Databases → every access is logged to Azure Log Analytics → Rules + Machine Learning + AI analyse it → results shown on a dashboard and pushed as email alerts. Runs entirely in Azure; no agents on your databases; all data synthetic.

Proof of Concept · Microsoft & Contoso Bank · All data is synthetic — no real personal information is used.  |  Deployed with a single command; can be removed just as easily.
